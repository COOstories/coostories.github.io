COO Stories

COO Stories brings together interesting people in ops leadership roles at fast-growing businesses.

Questions are asked and answered by the community over email and slack.

We meet for breakfast monthly at Ace Hotel in London to discuss a specific issue one of the group is currently facing.

Run by Kelsey Traher and Ben Gateley; made valuable by the many more who attend, contribute and share their experiences and wisdom with the rest of the community.

We currently grow through recommendations from our 120+ strong community. Have someone to recommend? You can do so here.
