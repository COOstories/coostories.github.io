Community Values

WE ARE A COMMUNITY

We are about face-to-face connections, and being part of something bigger than just us.

We recognise each other as our tribe, and feel energised by getting involved, contributing, and making friends.

Do

Be friendly

Help someone out

Participate - come to an event or join the conversation on email/slack

Make offline connections - Go for coffee or breakfast 1 on 1 with someone else

Don’t

Contribute nothing

WE LEARN FROM EACH OTHER

We learn from the collective experience in the community, and we know we can lean on each other when we need help.

We look for ways to help each other with advice and support, so we each get stronger as individuals.

Do

Ask for help when you need it

Learn and share

Don’t

Forget to ask for help (don’t always play the expert)

Forget to give back (always asking/taking)

WE LEAVE EGO AT THE DOOR

We’re humble about our achievements, and know we each still have a lot to learn.

We understand that wisdom comes from willingness to show vulnerability, and to grow from mistakes and failures.

Do

Show integrity

Trust each other

Share mistakes and failures

Don’t

Judge

Think you’ve learnt it all

Worry about asking an “obvious” question
